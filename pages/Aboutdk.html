About Digital Kosha

  • What is digital kosha?
    digital kosha is an Internet-based networking platform that helps promote upcoming South African musicians. Here musicians can share their music with the public and interact with their followers. Artists can market themselves by posting events where they will be performing. The public can access the music content on the platform through streaming and downloads.
  • The Legends In The Making

    The Legends in the Making – Spot a Legend competition, gives aspiring artists an opportunity to showcase their talent and collaborate internationally.
    In 2017, artists will be have an opportunity to physically and digitally expand their markets internationally, gaining a footprint in East Africa, South Africa and beyond.
    They will be introduced to entertainment industry experts who put them on platforms that will grow their brands.


    The structure of the competition gives you and opportunity to talk to your people and fans. It is platform for people to interact with your talent and to back it up through their votes!
