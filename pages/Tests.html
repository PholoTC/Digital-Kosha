The Legends In The Making

  • About SAL

    The Legends in the Making – Spot a Legend competition, gives aspiring artists an opportunity to showcase their talent and collaborate internationally. In 2017, artists will be have an opportunity to physically and digitally expand their markets internationally, gaining a footprint in East Africa, South Africa and beyond. They will be introduced to entertainment industry experts who put them on platforms that will grow their brands.


    The structure of the competition gives you and opportunity to talk to your people and fans. It is platform for people to interact with your talent and to back it up through their votes!

  • How It Works

    Step 1: Create your profile during public call to entry. Upload your music in audio or video format.
    Step 2: Upload your 2 min video / song.
    Step 3: Get your friends/ family & fans to vote for you.
    Step 4: Top musicians in the competition will get the opportunity to be curtain raisers at LIM events.
    Step 5: Top 5 artists will be profiled on the LIM Website.
    Step 6: Top artists win a tour to Tanzania/ Zanibar to perform at LIM Conference.How it works.

  • Conference

    The Legends in the Making Conference is an exciting 3-day action filled entrepreneurship and music conference. The inaugural Legends in the Making Conference is a culmination of the 7th O.R Tambo Uhuru Africa Initiative (formerly O.R Tambo Edu Tour) which is a 10 day educational tour to Tanzania focussing on liberation struggle sites linked to the African National Congress (ANC) ,currently South Africa’s governing political party.

  • The LIM Conference will involve :
    1. Company exhibitions.
    2. An exclusive pitch session where 8 entrepreneurs will present their business cases in front of a panel of captains of industry, entrepreneurs and investors to stand a chance to win ZAR 50 000.00 seed investment and incubation support.
    3.A power talk by international speakers
    4. Aspirant musicians will also battle it out to win financial prizes and support for their craft. They will also get an opportunity to showcase their music in front of international entertainment industry players..
    5. More announcements of activities will be announced in due course
